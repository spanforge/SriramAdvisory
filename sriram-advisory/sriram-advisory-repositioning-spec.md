# Sriram Advisory — Brand Repositioning Spec
**Goal:** Be known as India's Workforce Intelligence company, not an IT career guide company.

---

## The 10 High-Leverage Changes

---

### 1. Change the Homepage Headline

**Current:**
> AI Career Intelligence

**New Headline:**
> Career Intelligence for India

**New Subheading:**
> We help working professionals understand how technology, AI, automation, and market shifts are changing work — and what to do next.

**Keep this line (it's one of your strongest):**
> We don't guess your future. We model it.

---

### 2. Stop Leading With QA / BA / Java

**Current problem:** Visitors see QA, BA, Data Analyst, Java first → reads as an IT Career Site.

**Replace top-level sections with:**
- Research
- Assessments
- Reports
- Guides
- Intelligence Products

Role-specific guides (QA, BA, Java) move lower on the page — they become *examples of research*, not the brand's front door.

---

### 3. Add a "Workforce Intelligence" Category

Show the category. The products can come later.

```
Workforce Intelligence

  Banking Careers 2026        [Coming Soon]
  HR Careers 2026             [Coming Soon]
  Sales Careers 2026          [Coming Soon]
  Finance Careers 2026        [Coming Soon]
  Operations Careers 2026     [Coming Soon]
  Education Careers 2026      [Coming Soon]
```

> You don't need the products yet. You need the perception.

---

### 4. Create a Research Section

Highest ROI change on the site.

```
Research

  State of QA Careers 2026              [Coming Soon]
  State of Business Analysis 2026       [Coming Soon]
  State of BPO Careers 2026             [Coming Soon]
  State of Indian Careers 2026          [Coming Soon]
```

**Why:** People buy from researchers. Not marketers.

---

### 5. Add an "Industries We Track" Section

**Copy:**
> We analyze workforce trends across:

- Technology
- Banking
- Insurance
- Healthcare
- Retail
- Manufacturing
- Education
- Customer Support
- Finance
- Human Resources

**Effect:** Visitors stop thinking "only IT."

---

### 6. Reposition SA-AIRS™

**Current framing:**
> Career Risk Score

**New framing:**
> SA-AIRS™ Workforce Intelligence Framework

**Dimensions analyzed:**
- Automation Exposure
- Market Saturation
- Decision Complexity
- Human Dependency
- Future Demand

**Why it matters:** Repositions it from a product to a platform.

---

### 7. New Navigation Structure

**Current:**
```
Products   Guides   Tools
```

**New:**
```
Research   Assessments   Intelligence Reports   Guides   About
```

> The navigation teaches visitors what you are before they read a single word of copy.

---

### 8. Rewrite the About Page

**Current story (likely):**
> Helping professionals navigate AI.

**New story:**
> Sriram Advisory studies how work is changing in India. We combine workforce research, market intelligence, automation analysis, and structured assessment frameworks to help professionals make better career decisions.

This frames the company as operating in a much larger category.

---

### 9. Add a Metrics Banner

Place near the top of the homepage.

```
Our Mission: Analyze the future of work for —

  100 Crore    Working-Age Indians
  100+         Professions tracked
  20+          Industries covered
  1,000s       Career Pathways modeled
```

**Effect:** Immediately shifts scale perception.

---

### 10. Add One Flagship Product Placeholder

Not for sale. Just visible.

```
State of Indian Careers 2026
India's annual workforce intelligence report.

[Coming Soon]
```

> This single addition changes how the entire company is perceived.

---

## What NOT to Change

Do **not** remove:
- QA Guide
- BA Guide
- Java Guide
- BPO Guide
- AI Risk Rankings
- Career Intelligence Reports

These are **revenue-producing products.**

The strategic shift is this:

| Before | After |
|---|---|
| These guides are the brand | These guides are *evidence* of the research |
| Center of the site | Supporting examples, lower on the page |

---

## The Single Most Important Change

If only one thing gets done:

**Change the homepage from:**
> "AI Career Intelligence"

**To:**
> "Career Intelligence for India"

**And add:**
> Researching how work is changing across industries, professions, and regions in the age of AI.

That one change alone begins moving Sriram Advisory from *"IT career guide seller"* toward *"India's Workforce Intelligence company"* — the category worth building over the next 3–5 years.
